Sorry I don't know how you open it...

Nah kidding. To start Ifrit, double-click the Ifrit icon. Easy as pie, right?
Ifrit can pretty much alter any type of files, but are only meant to alter c0mXXX.dat files (from c0m001.dat to c0m143.dat. NOT c0m127.dat) from battle.fs. You should therefore be cautious not to open type of files.
